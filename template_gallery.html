The Raintale Template Gallery

To provide end users with the ability to tell stories in a variety of ways, Raintale supports various storytellers, presets, and templates. See the documentation for more details on how to use these.

This page contains screenshots and demos of:

  • the presets included with raintale
  • templates that are under consideration
